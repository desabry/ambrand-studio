# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Creative directors, startup founders, marketing leads, and business owners seeking professional branding and design services. They visit the site to evaluate the agency's capabilities, review portfolio work, and initiate contact for potential projects. The interface should evoke confidence, trust, and creative ambition.

## Product Purpose

Ambrand Studio's landing page serves as a portfolio and lead-generation tool. It showcases the agency's branding, visual identity, and design work to convert visitors into clients. Success means visitors understand the agency's caliber and are compelled to reach out.

## Brand Personality

Bold · Premium · Creative

Voice is confident and professional with an artistic edge. Tone shifts from authoritative (hero/CTA) to warm and approachable (testimonials/contact).

## Anti-references

Avoid over-designed or cluttered layouts. No excessive effects, no glassmorphism as default, no hero-metric templates. The design should feel premium but restrained — every element earns its place.

## Design Principles

1. **Confident restraint** — Bold doesn't mean busy. Use negative space and deliberate typography to convey premium quality.
2. **Show, don't tell** — Portfolio work speaks louder than copy. Let visuals lead.
3. **Red as conviction** — The brand red (#e31937 / #b81d28) is a commitment, not an accent. Use it generously and consistently.
4. **One job per section** — Every section has a clear purpose. No decorative elements that don't serve the goal.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance for text contrast. Consider reduced motion preferences.
