Brand Development 2026

Qahwa Coffee Roasters

The Challenge

Qahwa needed a brand identity that bridged the gap between traditional Arabic coffee heritage and modern, third-wave coffee culture. They required a complete structural overhaul of their visual presence to appeal to a younger demographic while maintaining their core historic values.

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive brand strategy that utilized a minimalist approach with deep, rich earth tones. By blending classic calligraphy elements with clean, sans-serif typography, we created packaging and digital touchpoints that feel both timeless and contemporary.
